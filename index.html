Our Story

The DeBerard name has been known around the stockyards for over a century. Fred C. DeBerard was known as the “King of Cattle” when he came into town and began showing cattle in the early 1900s. He is remembered by a saying he would frequently share, “good cattle pay the way”.

In 1951 Fred C. DeBerard was honored as the Man of the Year in Livestock and was inducted into the Hall of Fame in 1979.

Our family has had the opportunity to become more involved in the cattle industry and we are excited to begin our expedition with Herefords. My wife and I hope to leave a legacy for our son Hunter and if we are lucky enough maybe one day, we will earn our place at NWSS like my great great grandfather.
